Requirements for a Saudi Arabia Visa

Before you apply for a Saudi Arabia visa online, you'll need to make sure you have all the required documents in order.

The list of documents you'll need includes: your passport, a copy of your passport, a copy of your travel itinerary, a copy of your Saudi Arabia visa application form, a recent passport-sized photograph, and proof of your financial situation.

If you're applying for a business visa, you'll also need to provide a letter from your employer confirming your position and stating that you're traveling for business purposes.

Make sure you have all the required documents before you start the online application process, as it will save you time and hassle in the long run.

Saudi Arabia eVisa

The eVisa for Saudi Arabia is an electronic visa that you can apply for online. It's a hassle-free way to get your visa, and you don't have to go to the embassy or consulate.

The eVisa is valid for tourism and business purposes, and it's valid for 90 days from the date of issue. You can stay in Saudi Arabia for up to 30 days.

The application process is simple. You just need to provide your personal information, passport information, and travel details. You'll also need to upload a digital photo of yourself.
